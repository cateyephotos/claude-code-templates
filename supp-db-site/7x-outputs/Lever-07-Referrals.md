## Referral System Design (Ready to Deploy)

### Client Tiering (Activate When Users Exist)

| Tier | Definition | Follow-Up | Ask Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rainmakers | Bought 2+ products, high engagement | Monthly personal email | After any positive interaction |
| Champions | Bought 1 product, active user | Quarterly check-in | After visible win (e.g., good AI analysis result) |
| Rising Stars | Free user, high engagement (80%+ email opens) | Include in appreciation promos | After they consume a guide or run 3+ analyses |

### Referral Reward Structure

| Who | Reward | Mechanics |
|---|---|---|
| Referrer | 1 free month of Pro | Unique referral link, credit applied after friend signs up |
| Friend | 7-day free Pro trial | Access via referral link, no credit card required |

**Why these rewards:**
- Pro month for referrer: tangible value, costs you nothing in hard dollars (marginal cost near zero for SaaS)
- 7-day trial for friend: removes all risk, lets the product sell itself
- No cash rewards: maintains "no commercial interests" brand positioning

### Introduction Handling

When a referrer agrees:
1. Send them a pre-written message they can forward (reduce effort to zero)
2. Draft: "Hey [Friend], I've been using this supplement database that rates every supplement against clinical trial evidence. Thought you'd find it useful — here's 7 days free: [LINK]"
3. Follow up with referrer within 48 hours: "Thanks for sharing — [Friend] signed up. Your free Pro month is active."

### Referral Sprint (7-Day Activation)

**Run this sprint when you have 25+ users:**

| Day | Action | Target |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Email all Rainmakers with personal referral ask | 5-10 users |
| 2 | Follow up with Champions who opened Day 1 email | 10-15 users |
| 3 | Post in any community where you're active: "Built a supplement evidence database — free 7-day trial for anyone interested" | Community |
| 4 | Email Rising Stars with "share with a friend" CTA | 15-20 users |
| 5 | Personal follow-up with anyone who clicked referral link but didn't send | 5-10 users |
| 6 | Thank-you email to everyone who referred (whether successful or not) | All referrers |
| 7 | Tally results, update tracking, note what worked | — |

### Follow-Up & Feedback Loop

After every successful referral:
1. **Immediate:** "Thanks — [Friend]'s trial is active. Your free month starts now."
2. **After 7 days:** "[Friend] converted to Pro!" or "[Friend]'s trial ended — thanks for sharing regardless"
