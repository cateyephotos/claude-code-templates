## SECTION 5: Objections, Anxiety & "Not Me" Repair

### Point 23: Directly Handles Objections + Buyer Anxieties
**Score: 5/10**

**Current State:** 10 objections identified but no public-facing FAQ or objection-handling copy.

**Full Objection Handling (for FAQ section and sales pages):**

> **"I can find this stuff free on Google."**
> You can find *information* on Google. You can't find *graded evidence*. Google gives you 10 affiliate blogs, 3 Reddit threads, and a WebMD page from 2019. We give you a systematic evidence classification across 471 papers with clinical dosages and interaction warnings. That's the difference between information and a decision framework.

> **"Examine.com already does this."**
> Examine is excellent. We're complementary, not competitive. What we add: a 4-tier evidence grading system, an AI-powered Stack Analyzer that evaluates your personal combination, interaction warnings across your full stack, and $9 health-goal guides with decision matrices. If you use Examine, you'll find SupplementDB fills the gaps.

> **"I'll just ask my doctor."**
> You should. But most physicians have 15-20 hours of nutrition training across their entire education. Supplements are not their specialty. SupplementDB gives you (and your doctor) a quick evidence reference — bring it to your next appointment.

> **"$9 is too much for a PDF."**
> One month of a Tier 4 supplement costs $15-40. This guide tells you which ones to stop buying. It pays for itself the first month.

> **"I only take 2-3 supplements. I don't need this."**
> That's actually the ideal use case. Fewer supplements means each one matters more. A $9 guide ensures those 2-3 are the right 2-3.

> **"How do I know this is trustworthy?"**
> Every claim links to its source paper on PubMed or Cochrane. Our evidence grading methodology is published. We receive zero industry funding. We don't sell supplements. If we're wrong about something, you can check our citations and tell us.

> **"I don't have time to read a guide."**
> The Decision Matrix is on one page. Look at it for 2 minutes. That's the minimum viable use of this guide, and it's enough to make a better decision than 95% of supplement buyers.

> **"This is just another supplement marketing site."**
> We don't sell supplements. We don't accept industry funding. We rate 22 supplements as Tier 4 ("Preliminary/Limited evidence"). No marketing site would do that.

> **"I have health conditions — can this help?"**
> We don't provide medical advice. We provide evidence classifications. If you have a health condition, use our database as a research starting point and discuss findings with your healthcare provider. That's the responsible use case.

> **"Can I cancel?"**
> Pro subscriptions: cancel anytime, effective at end of billing period. PDF guides: one-time purchase, 7-day refund if it doesn't meet expectations. No hoops.
