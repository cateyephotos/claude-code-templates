## 1. Business Identity

SupplementDB is the evidence-first supplement research platform that rates every supplement claim against peer-reviewed clinical trials — and sells zero supplements. It organizes 93 supplements across 13 categories using a transparent 4-tier evidence classification (Strong → Moderate → Preliminary → Emerging), backed by 471+ papers from PubMed, Cochrane Library, and ClinicalTrials.gov. The platform includes an AI-powered Stack Analyzer that generates personalized supplement recommendations for 13 health goals. Positioning: Mass Appeal. Tone: calm authority with dry humor. Zero affiliate links, zero industry funding, zero supplement sales.
