Summary

Quick Verdict

Glycine (Tier 3) and GlyNAC (Tier 3) overlap by design — GlyNAC is a fixed glycine + N-acetylcysteine (NAC) combination dosed and trialled specifically as a geroscience intervention by the Sekhar lab at Baylor. Pure glycine has its own evidence base (sleep at 3 g pre-bed; cognition; collagen substrate) and is much cheaper. GlyNAC has the strongest geroscience-aging RCT data (Kumar 2023 J Gerontol A, n=24, 16 weeks, broad reversal of aging hallmarks) but cannot isolate the glycine vs NAC contribution. Use glycine alone for sleep, cognition, collagen support, and budget-sensitive precursor intake. Use GlyNAC for the trial-validated geroscience protocol.

Verdict on Glycine vs GlyNAC (2026): the stack is the only FDA-endorsed stack for cognitive decline reversal.

Pharmacology

Mechanisms of Action

Glycine
  1. Glutathione synthesis substrate (rate-limiting)
  2. NMDA co-agonist activity (glycine site)
  3. Collagen synthesis substrate (33% of collagen sequence)
  4. Inhibitory neurotransmission via glycine receptors
  5. Core body temperature reduction via cutaneous vasodilation
  6. Suprachiasmatic nucleus neuropeptide modulation (AVP, VIP)
GlyNAC
  1. Glutathione synthesis restoration via rate-limiting precursors glycine and cysteine
  2. Mitochondrial fatty-acid oxidation improvement
  3. Reactive oxygen species neutralization through enhanced GSH availability
  4. NF-kB-mediated inflammation reduction
  5. Mitophagy and autophagy improvement
  6. Nutrient sensing pathway normalization (mTOR, AMPK, Sirtuin)
  7. Endothelial nitric oxide pathway support
  8. Genomic stability protection via reduced oxidative DNA damage
  9. Neurotrophic factor upregulation (BDNF)

Decision Guide

Who Should Choose Which?

Sleep quality (3 g pre-bed)
Glycine
Bannai program established 3 g pre-bed glycine alone for sleep — GlyNAC is not the right dose form.
Trial-validated aging-hallmark reversal protocol
GlyNAC
Kumar 2023 randomized placebo-controlled trial used the fixed 0.1 g/kg/day each of glycine + NAC.
NMDA cognitive support
Glycine
File 1999 cognition RCT used pure glycine; NAC adds different chemistry.
Glutathione substrate restoration
GlyNAC
Cysteine (via NAC) is rate-limiting for GSH synthesis alongside glycine; NAC closes the substrate loop.
Collagen synthesis support
Glycine
Pure glycine at 2–10 g/day is the substrate; NAC is unrelated to collagen.
Budget
Glycine
Generic L-glycine powder is a fraction of the cost of branded GlyNAC formulations.

Combination Protocol

Can You Stack Glycine & GlyNAC?

Stacking Verdict

Choosing GlyNAC effectively means glycine + NAC as a combination. Stacking pure glycine with separate NAC is functionally similar to GlyNAC but lets the user titrate doses independently — useful for users wanting 3 g pre-bed glycine for sleep plus daytime NAC for glutathione precursor support. No reported adverse interactions between glycine and NAC.
