## Inclusion Criteria

Papers were included if they met ALL of the following:

1. **Study Design:** Systematic review, meta-analysis, or network meta-analysis published 2024-01-01 to 2026-03-05
2. **Intervention:** Acetyl-L-carnitine (ALCAR) as primary or prominently named intervention (not merely one of many unspecified supplements)
3. **Population:** Human supplementation studies (not animal-only, not biomarker/metabolomics)
4. **Outcomes:** Quantitative outcomes reported (effect sizes, SUCRA rankings, pooled estimates, or clinically measurable endpoints)
5. **Language:** English
6. **Novelty:** Not already present in the existing enhanced citations file

## Exclusion Criteria

Papers were excluded if they met ANY of the following:

1. ALCAR/carnitine used only as a biomarker, metabolite, or biochemical endpoint (not supplemented)
2. ALCAR is a minor or unnamed component of a multicomponent intervention with no ALCAR-specific outcomes
3. Animal-only or in-vitro-only studies (including animal systematic reviews)
4. L-carnitine-general meta-analysis without ALCAR-specific subgroup analysis or results
5. Case reports, editorials, commentaries, conference abstracts, or Mendelian randomization studies
6. Drug poisoning, critical care, or acute clinical settings (not supplementation for health/wellness)
7. Duplicate of paper already in enhanced citations file or already assigned to another supplement
8. Non-systematic narrative review without quantitative synthesis
