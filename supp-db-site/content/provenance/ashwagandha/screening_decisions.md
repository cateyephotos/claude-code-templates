# Screening Decisions: Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-05

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (clinical trials, observational studies, or meta-analyses thereof)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal (2024 or later)
3. Available DOI and PMID
4. Relevant to ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) root extract's claimed benefits, mechanisms, or safety
5. Meta-analysis, systematic review, or large RCT preferred
6. Adds NEW information beyond existing database citations
7. Oral supplementation route (excludes topical, IV, or non-standardized preparations)
8. Ashwagandha as primary intervention (not peripheral component in multi-supplement protocols)

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Conference abstracts only (no full publication)
2. Duplicate publications of same dataset or preprint-final pairs
3. Narrative reviews without novel meta-analytic data or quantitative synthesis
4. Ashwagandha as peripheral/minor component in network meta-analyses of broad supplement categories
5. Animal or in-vitro studies without human data
6. Studies where ashwagandha effects cannot be isolated from multi-ingredient formulations
7. Superseded by more comprehensive or more recent meta-analysis on the same outcome domain
8. Comment/letter/editorial format without original data
