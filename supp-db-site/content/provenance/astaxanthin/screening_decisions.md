## Inclusion Criteria

Citations were included if they met ALL of the following:
1. **Human subjects only** — animal studies, in vitro studies, and cell line studies excluded from all domains
2. **Astaxanthin specificity** — study must evaluate astaxanthin as the primary intervention (not krill oil, carotenoid blends, or multi-ingredient products where astaxanthin cannot be isolated as the active component)
3. **PMID verification** — citation must have a verifiable PMID; no-PMID citations excluded unless they are regulatory documents with DOI
4. **Domain relevance** — citation must provide substantive evidence for the assigned domain (mechanisms, benefits, safety, or dosage)
5. **No cross-domain PMID duplication** — each PMID appears in exactly one domain
6. **Publication in peer-reviewed journal** — conference abstracts, preprints, and non-peer-reviewed sources excluded
