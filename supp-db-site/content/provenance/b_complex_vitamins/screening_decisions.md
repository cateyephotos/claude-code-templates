### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (for clinical/epidemiological evidence; in vitro only acceptable for mechanistic sections)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal with verifiable DOI and PMID
3. Directly relevant to B-complex vitamins, individual B vitamins, or folate/homocysteine pathway
4. Original research article, systematic review, or meta-analysis (not editorials or case reports)
5. Findings accurately extractable from abstract (full text not required for inclusion)

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Papers resolving to a completely unrelated field (wrong PMID — excluded by default)
2. Conference abstracts without full publication
3. Non-English without English abstract
4. Retracted papers
5. Animal studies for benefit/safety sections (acceptable only for mechanism sections with explicit labeling)
6. Duplicate datasets (e.g., subgroup reports from an already-included trial)
