## Evaluation Framework

### Primary Framework: GRADE
The Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation (GRADE) framework was applied to assess certainty of evidence across five domains:
1. **Risk of bias** — Internal validity of individual studies
2. **Inconsistency** — Heterogeneity across studies
3. **Indirectness** — Applicability to the population/intervention of interest
4. **Imprecision** — Width of confidence intervals / sample adequacy
5. **Publication bias** — Likelihood of missing studies

## Evaluation Limitations

1. Individual RCT quality within meta-analyses was not independently assessed — relied on MA authors' quality assessments
2. Mechanism papers include animal/in vitro data which may not fully translate to human effects
3. Many RCTs within meta-analyses were conducted in Asian populations — generalizability to other ethnicities needs further study
