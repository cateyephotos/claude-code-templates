# Screening Decisions: Calcium Alpha-Ketoglutarate (Ca-AKG)
## Pipeline Run: 2026-04-19

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Paper is explicitly about alpha-ketoglutarate or calcium alpha-ketoglutarate as the primary intervention or target molecule.
2. Published in a peer-reviewed journal (or preprint with DOI, if landmark).
3. Provides one of: human clinical data, mechanistic insight, preclinical lifespan/healthspan data, or authoritative human review.
4. Available DOI AND PMID (both required for the citation-integrity gate).

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Papers on AKG as a tumor metabolite marker (oncology focus) without supplementation relevance.
2. Veterinary-feed studies (e.g., fattening lambs — PMID 41764984) — not clinically translatable.
3. Conference abstracts and retractions.
4. Papers on arginine alpha-ketoglutarate (AAKG, athletic pump) or ornithine alpha-ketoglutarate (OKG, burn/wound ICU nutrition) without direct relevance to longevity or epigenetic reprogramming.
