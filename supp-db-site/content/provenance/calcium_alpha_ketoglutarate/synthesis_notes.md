# Research Synthesis: Calcium Alpha-Ketoglutarate (Ca-AKG)
## Pipeline Run: 2026-04-19

### Synthesis Methodology
- **Approach:** Narrative synthesis. Quantitative pooling is not possible with a single human observational study and preclinical heterogeneity.
- **Effect-size metric:** Paper-reported means (e.g., mean years of DNA-methylation age change); preclinical median lifespan extension reported as per authors.
- **Confidence assessment:** GRADE + Mode 1 tier decision tree.

### Mechanism Synthesis

#### Mechanism: ATP synthase / TOR inhibition
- **Sources:** Chin 2014 (24828042)
- **Convergent evidence:** Direct binding of α-KG to ATP5B, decreased ATP production, TOR pathway downregulation, lifespan extension in C. elegans, conservation in mammalian cells.
- **Divergent evidence:** None material identified.
- **Strength assignment:** Strong — landmark *Nature* paper, strong mechanism-to-phenotype chain.
- **Tissue target:** Mitochondria / whole-organism metabolism.

#### Mechanism: Histone demethylation via α-KG-dependent dioxygenases
- **Sources:** Wang 2020 (33154378)
- **Convergent evidence:** α-KG is the required cofactor for Jumonji-domain histone demethylases. AKG supplementation lowered H3K9me3/H3K27me3 at osteogenic loci and restored aged MSC differentiation.
- **Strength assignment:** Strong — direct ChIP-seq and RNA-seq evidence tying AKG to epigenetic remodeling.
- **Tissue target:** Bone marrow mesenchymal stromal cells (generalizable to any tissue expressing α-KG-dependent demethylases).

#### Mechanism: Mitophagy and oxidative-stress regulation
- **Sources:** Liu 2023 (36924682)
- **Convergent evidence:** Human OA cartilage shows decreased α-KG; intra-articular AKG in mice reduced mitochondrial ROS and promoted PINK1/Parkin mitophagy.
- **Strength assignment:** Moderate — single paper, translational but not replicated yet.
- **Tissue target:** Articular cartilage / chondrocytes (generalizable to other aged tissues with mitochondrial dysfunction).

### Benefit Synthesis

#### Benefit: Biological-age reduction (DNA-methylation clock)
- **Sources:** Demidenko 2021 (34847066)
- **Effect size reported:** −8 years mean TruAge reduction over ~7 months in n=42 Rejuvant users.
- **Replication:** None (single retrospective cohort).
- **Clinical-significance threshold:** Meaningful if replicated in an RCT; current data hypothesis-generating.
- **Confidence:** Low (retrospective, uncontrolled, vitamin co-formulation).

#### Benefit: Mammalian lifespan and healthspan extension
- **Sources:** Asadi Shahmirzadi 2020 (32877690), supported by Chin 2014 (24828042)
- **Effect size:** Median lifespan extension with compressed morbidity in aged C57BL/6 mice; more pronounced in females. ~50% lifespan extension in C. elegans.
- **Replication:** Yes — across species and independent labs.
- **Confidence:** High for preclinical; translation to humans remains the open question.

#### Benefit: Muscle / wound healing / immune effects (historical OKG literature)
- **Sources:** Gyanwali 2022 review (34952764) summarizing 1980s–1990s OKG RCTs.
- **Effect size:** Heterogeneous across older studies; review concludes "supportive but inadequate by modern standards."
- **Replication:** Historical only; not contemporary RCT data on Ca-AKG.
- **Confidence:** Moderate as historical signal; low as direct support for longevity claims.

### Safety Synthesis

#### Safety Aspect: General tolerability at 1000 mg/day
- **Sources:** Demidenko 2021 (34847066); Gyanwali 2022 (34952764).
- **Adverse-event aggregation:** Retrospective cohort reported no serious adverse events; historical OKG literature supported good tolerability at supplemental doses.
- **Risk-level determination:** Low — but caveated by retrospective and historical-only safety reporting. Pregnancy/lactation, severe renal impairment, and hypercalcemia are theoretical contraindications flagged in the monograph.

### Dosage Synthesis
- **Optimal dosage determination:** 1000 mg/day Ca-AKG is the only human-tested dose in the aging/longevity context (Demidenko retrospective). The ABLE trial will provide prospective dosing rigor.
- **Duration evidence:** ~7 months observational; no evidence-based upper duration yet.
- **Regulatory/safety note:** Ornithine AKG is pharmacopoeial and has long safety history in clinical nutrition — relevant but not directly transferable.

### Cross-Study Patterns
- **Consistent findings:** α-KG modulates canonical longevity pathways (TOR, epigenetic regulation, mitochondrial quality control). Preclinical lifespan extension is replicated.
- **Contradictory findings:** None within the included set.
- **Dose-response:** Not established in humans.
- **Population-specific effects:** Female mice showed stronger effects (Asadi Shahmirzadi 2020); ABLE trial targets biologically-older middle-aged adults.

### Limitations of This Synthesis
1. Single human observational study drives the biological-age claim.
2. No completed human RCT results — the Lim 2025 paper is feasibility-only.
3. Co-formulation (vitamins A+D) in Demidenko 2021 confounds attribution.
4. Narrow demographic in published human data (self-selected Rejuvant users).
