## Pipeline Run: 2026-04-19

2. How many RCTs are published? → **0 completed human RCTs on Ca-AKG for aging endpoints**
3. Decision-tree branch: "NO RCTs (only observational/case studies) → Tier 4"
4. **Exception applied:** "Strong mechanistic plausibility + active clinical trials → Tier 3"
   - Active clinical trial: ABLE (Lim 2025 PMID 40819772)
   - Strong mechanistic plausibility: 3 independent high-impact mechanism papers (TOR/ATP synthase, histone demethylation, mitophagy)
   - One published human observational signal (Demidenko 2021): 8-year biological-age reduction

**Decision:** **Tier 3 — Emerging Evidence** — warranted by the mechanistic depth and active human trial, but capped short of Tier 2 because no placebo-controlled RCT has reported efficacy.

### Research Maturity: Emerging
Human RCT evidence is not yet published. Mechanistic and preclinical data are mature, but the translation gap remains the rate-limiting step.

### Evidence Gaps Identified
1. No placebo-controlled RCT of Ca-AKG on aging endpoints (being addressed by ABLE).
2. No dose-response data in humans.
3. No long-term (>12 month) human safety data at supplemental doses.
4. Independent replication of the Demidenko biological-age signal is needed using multiple methylation clocks.
