# CoQ10 (ID: 18) — Synthesis Notes
**Date:** 2026-03-05

## Key Insights

### Dose-Response Confirmation
The Talebi 2024 and Daei 2024 MAs independently confirm dose-response relationships in different domains (exercise muscle protection and endothelial function). This cross-domain dose-response validation is a strong signal for biological plausibility.

### Evidence Domain Expansion
Prior to this update, CoQ10 evidence was primarily cardiovascular (heart failure, BP) with some statin myopathy data. The update expands into:
- **Exercise performance/recovery** — 28-RCT GRADE-assessed MA with clear dose-response
- **Vascular endothelial function** — 12-study dose-response MA
- **Metabolic health** — umbrella review for glycemic/lipid effects

### Tier Correction
The previous Tier 3 designation appears to have been conservative given the existing evidence. With Xu 2024 (33-RCT HF MA showing 36% mortality reduction) already in the file, plus now 3 additional high-quality MAs, Tier 2 is well-supported. The duplicate file (18_coq10_enhanced.js) had an inflated Tier 1 with fabricated PMIDs — this was archived.

### Clinical Translation
CoQ10 dosing guidance is increasingly evidence-based:
- Heart failure: 100-300mg/day (well-established from Q-SYMBIO and meta-analyses)
- Exercise recovery: dose-dependent, each 100mg/day adds incremental protection
- Endothelial function: dose-dependent (meta-regression confirmed)
- Metabolic: effective across dose range for glycemic parameters
