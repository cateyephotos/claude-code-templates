# Screening Decisions: Creatine
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-04

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (clinical trials, observational studies, or meta-analyses thereof)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal (2025 or later)
3. Available DOI and PMID
4. Relevant to creatine monohydrate's claimed benefits, mechanisms, or safety
5. Meta-analysis, systematic review, or large RCT preferred
6. Adds NEW information beyond existing 18 database citations
7. Oral supplementation route (excludes IV creatine phosphate or pharmaceutical derivatives)

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Conference abstracts only (no full publication)
2. Duplicate publications of same dataset
3. Narrative reviews without novel meta-analytic data or quantitative synthesis
4. Non-supplement forms (e.g., creatine phosphate sodium as pharmaceutical agent)
5. Animal or in-vitro studies without human data
6. Studies not specifically about creatine supplementation (e.g., creatine kinase biomarker studies)
7. Multi-supplement protocols where creatine effects cannot be isolated
8. Comment/letter format without original data
