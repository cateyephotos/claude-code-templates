### Search Rationale
A single PubMed query was used targeting creatine in the title field combined with meta-analysis or systematic review publication types, as creatine terminology is consistent in the literature (unlike omega-3/fish oil dual nomenclature). The date filter was set to 2025/08 to capture all evidence published since the previous update date of 2025-08-18. The Consensus API query was designed to specifically target the emerging cognitive/neuroprotective domain of creatine research, using quality filters to surface high-impact papers. Three representative results from Consensus (Kaplinska et al. 2025, Tomczyk et al. 2025, Fabiano & Candow 2025) were identified as narrative reviews and subsequently excluded during screening for lacking novel meta-analytic data.
