### Research Maturity: **Emerging**

**Justification:** Mechanism science is mature and high-impact (Cell Metab 2025 target identification), but the human clinical evidence base is thin — one completed pilot RCT, one published protocol, and one large prospective cohort. Multiple ongoing trials (ErgMS, others on ClinicalTrials.gov) suggest active translational momentum, but at present the clinical maturity is "Emerging".

### Evidence Gaps Identified
1. **No completed RCTs in healthy adults** — all completed clinical evidence is in MCI subjects (Yau pilot) or epidemiological cohorts
2. **No dose-response RCTs** — ErgMS protocol (5 vs 30 mg/day) will be the first
3. **Effect size on hard human endpoints is uncertain** — cohort HRs are derived from quartile/SD comparisons of plasma levels, not from supplementation
4. **Bioavailability variability between sources** — synthetic L-ergothioneine vs mushroom-derived has not been rigorously compared in a head-to-head RCT
5. **Long-term safety beyond 12 months in supplemented populations not established**
