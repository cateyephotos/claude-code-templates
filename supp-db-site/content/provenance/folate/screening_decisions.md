# Screening Decisions: Folate / Vitamin B9 (ID 23)
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-06 | Mode: Mode 2 — Evidence Update

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (clinical/epidemiological); in vitro/mechanistic acceptable in mechanisms section with explicit labeling
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal with verifiable DOI and PMID
3. Directly relevant to folate (folic acid/5-MTHF/L-methylfolate) supplementation, deficiency, or metabolism
4. Original research article, systematic review, meta-analysis, or clinical practice guideline
5. PMID resolves to the claimed paper (verified via PubMed MCP)
6. Findings accurately extractable from abstract

### Exclusion Criteria
1. PMID not found in PubMed or resolves to wrong paper
2. Conference abstracts without full journal publication
3. Non-English without English abstract
4. Retracted papers
5. Animal or in vitro studies in benefit/safety sections
6. Duplicate datasets from already-included trials
7. Papers assigned to wrong supplement (Phase 3B-6 women's health content that was erroneously in the prior file)
