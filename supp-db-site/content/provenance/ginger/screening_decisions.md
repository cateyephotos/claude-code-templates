## Inclusion Criteria

Citations were included if they met ALL of the following:
1. **Human subjects only** — animal studies, in vitro studies, and cell line studies excluded from all domains (exception: regulatory safety reviews that synthesize toxicological frameworks may include preclinical data in the safety domain only)
2. **Ginger specificity** — study must evaluate ginger (Zingiber officinale) as the primary intervention; multi-ingredient products where ginger cannot be isolated as the active component excluded
3. **PMID verification** — citation must have a verifiable PMID; regulatory monographs with ISBN/reference ID accepted for dosage domain
4. **Domain relevance** — citation must provide substantive evidence for the assigned domain (mechanisms, benefits, safety, or dosage)
5. **No cross-domain PMID duplication** — each PMID appears in exactly one domain (cross-domain duplicates from prior versions resolved in Mode 2+ processing)
6. **Publication in peer-reviewed source** — conference abstracts, preprints, and non-peer-reviewed sources excluded; regulatory monographs (Commission E) accepted for dosage domain
