### Aggregate Quality Metrics
- **Pure-glycine RCTs:** 2 (Bannai 2012b sleep; File 1999 cognition); 1 combination-supplement RCT (Langan-Evans 2023)
- **GlyNAC trials cited for mechanistic inference:** 1 placebo-controlled RCT (Kumar 2023, n=24) + 2 open-label pilots
- **Cross-lab independence:** Ajinomoto/Bannai (Japan), Guy's Hospital London (File), Liverpool John Moores (Langan-Evans), Newcastle Australia (Dunstan), Baylor (Sekhar program), Charles University Prague — adequate but the GlyNAC literature is dominated by a single group
- **Recency:** 6 of 12 papers from 2020–2023 — actively expanding
- **Funding diversity:** Industry (Ajinomoto for several Bannai papers, Pure Food Norway for some), academic (most), Baylor/USDA for the Sekhar GlyNAC program
