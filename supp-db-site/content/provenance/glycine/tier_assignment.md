**Justification:** Mature mechanism science + small but converging RCT base + active geroscience programme via GlyNAC.

### Evidence Gaps Identified
1. No completed pure-glycine RCT in older adults at the trial-validated GlyNAC dose
2. No completed sleep RCT with polysomnography rather than subjective measures
3. Cognition replication of File 1999 not yet performed in modern cohort
4. Long-term safety beyond GlyNAC's 16-week protocol not formally established
