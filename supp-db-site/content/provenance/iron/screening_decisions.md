# Screening Decisions: Iron
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-04

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal
3. Available DOI and PMID
4. Directly relevant to iron supplementation efficacy, safety, or cognitive/performance outcomes
5. Systematic review, meta-analysis, or umbrella review (highest evidence levels)
6. Published 2024-2026

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Multi-nutrient combination studies where iron is not the primary intervention
2. Food fortification studies (not direct supplementation)
3. Conference abstracts only
4. Studies focused on malaria-endemic populations (too specialized context)
5. Database description papers without clinical findings
6. Dietary pattern studies (not supplementation)
