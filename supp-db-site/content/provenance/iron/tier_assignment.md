### Research Maturity: Mature
**Justification:** Iron is one of the most extensively studied micronutrients globally. Evidence spans from landmark studies (1985) to 2026 umbrella review. Multiple evidence levels including umbrella reviews, meta-analyses, and network meta-analyses. Well-established mechanisms, dosing, and safety profile.

### Evidence Gaps Identified
1. Limited data on long-term cognitive outcomes of iron supplementation in non-anemic adults
2. Dose-response relationships for cognitive/psychiatric outcomes not yet established
3. Optimal duration of supplementation for sports performance benefit needs clarification
4. Head-to-head comparisons of oral iron forms for cognitive outcomes lacking
