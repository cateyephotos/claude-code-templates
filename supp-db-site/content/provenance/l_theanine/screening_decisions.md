# Screening Decisions: L-Theanine (gamma-Glutamylethylamide)
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-05

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (clinical trials, observational studies, or meta-analyses thereof)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal (2024 or later)
3. Available DOI and PMID
4. Relevant to L-theanine's claimed benefits, mechanisms, or safety
5. Meta-analysis, systematic review, or large RCT preferred
6. Adds NEW information beyond existing database citations
7. Oral supplementation route (excludes topical, IV, or non-standardized preparations)
8. L-theanine as primary intervention or clearly isolable from combination interventions
9. Studies evaluating L-theanine+caffeine accepted only if L-theanine-alone effects are separately reported or the combination is a recognized standard intervention

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Conference abstracts only (no full publication)
2. Duplicate publications of same dataset or preprint-final pairs
3. Narrative reviews without novel meta-analytic data or quantitative synthesis
4. L-theanine as peripheral/minor component in network meta-analyses of broad supplement categories
5. Animal or in-vitro studies without human data
6. Studies where L-theanine effects cannot be isolated from multi-ingredient formulations (unless L-theanine+caffeine is the specific combination studied)
7. Superseded by more comprehensive or more recent meta-analysis on the same outcome domain
8. Comment/letter/editorial format without original data
9. Sample size < 20 total participants (insufficient statistical power for meaningful conclusions)
10. Sport-specific performance studies with non-generalizable populations (e.g., elite athletes in niche disciplines)
