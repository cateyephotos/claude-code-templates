# Screening Decisions: Lion's Mane Mushroom (ID 11) - Mode 2 Evidence Update

**Date:** 2026-03-05
**Supplement:** Lion's Mane Mushroom (Hericium erinaceus)
**Database ID:** 11
**Total Papers Screened:** 9

---

## Inclusion Criteria

1. Systematic review, meta-analysis, or high-quality RCT focused on Hericium erinaceus
2. Includes human clinical data
3. Lion's Mane is the primary or sole intervention under study
4. Published 2024-2026
5. Peer-reviewed or registered protocol (e.g., PROSPERO)

## Exclusion Criteria

1. Preclinical only (animal/cell models without human data)
2. Lion's Mane is a minor component among multiple interventions
3. Narrative review without systematic methodology
4. Underpowered or acute single-dose studies
5. Duplicate publication

---

## INCLUDED (1)

### Menon et al. 2025
- **PMID:** 40959699
- **Title:** PRISMA Systematic Review of Hericium erinaceus
- **Study Design:** PRISMA Systematic Review with PROSPERO registration (CRD42024571250)
- **Scope:** 5 RCTs + 3 prospective clinical trials (PCTs) + 15 laboratory studies
- **Quality Assessment Tool:** ROBIS (Risk of Bias in Systematic Reviews)
- **Key Finding:** Combined weighted mean MMSE improvement of +1.17 points
- **Inclusion Rationale:** Most comprehensive Lion's Mane SR to date. PROSPERO-registered, PRISMA-compliant, includes human clinical data across multiple outcome domains (cognition, mood, gut health, neuroprotection). Uses validated quality assessment tool (ROBIS). Directly relevant to updating evidence tier.
- **Decision:** **INCLUDE**

## EXCLUDED (8)

### Spangenberg et al. 2025
- **PMID:** 40626304
- **Study Design:** Preclinical systematic review
- **Topic:** Erinacines in cellular and rodent models
- **Exclusion Reason:** Preclinical SR only, no human data. Focused exclusively on erinacine compounds in cell culture and animal models. Does not meet inclusion criterion #2 (human clinical data).
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: preclinical only)

### Gong et al. 2025
- **PMID:** 40289452
- **Study Design:** Systematic review
- **Topic:** Multiple fungi for antidepressant effects
- **Exclusion Reason:** Lion's Mane is one of several mushroom species reviewed. Not a focused H. erinaceus review. Does not meet inclusion criterion #3 (primary or sole intervention).
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: LM minor component)

### Mohd Hisam & Wong 2024
- **PMID:** 38539908
- **Study Design:** Review
- **Topic:** SCA3 (spinocerebellar ataxia type 3) traditional Chinese medicine herbal remedies
- **Exclusion Reason:** Lion's Mane is a minor component within a broader TCM herbal remedies review. Preclinical only. Does not meet inclusion criteria #2 or #3.
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: LM minor component, preclinical only)

### Taib et al. 2026
- **PMID:** 41562932
- **Study Design:** Review
- **Topic:** Mushrooms for psychoneuroimmunology (PNI)
- **Exclusion Reason:** Multiple mushroom species reviewed (11 total), Lion's Mane accounts for only 2 of 11 studies discussed. Preclinical focus only. Does not meet inclusion criteria #2 or #3.
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: LM 2 of 11 studies, preclinical only)

### Nieman et al. 2023
- **PMID:** 37561965
- **Study Design:** Scoping review
- **Topic:** 16 dietary ingredients for cognitive function
- **Exclusion Reason:** Scoping review covering 16 different dietary ingredients. Lion's Mane is a minor component. Does not provide focused analysis of H. erinaceus evidence. Does not meet inclusion criterion #3.
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: LM minor component among 16 ingredients)

### Komon 2024
- **Source:** Consensus
- **Study Design:** Narrative review
- **Topic:** Lion's Mane cognitive effects
- **Exclusion Reason:** Small narrative review covering only 4 studies. No systematic methodology (no PRISMA, no registered protocol). Limited scope does not add beyond Menon 2025. Does not meet inclusion criterion #1 (systematic methodology).
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: narrative review, limited scope)

### Surendran 2025
- **Source:** Consensus
- **Study Design:** Acute RCT
- **Topic:** Single-dose Lion's Mane cognitive effects
- **Exclusion Reason:** Acute single-dose RCT with n=18 participants. Mostly null results across cognitive endpoints. Severely underpowered. Does not meet inclusion criterion threshold for meaningful evidence contribution.
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: underpowered acute study, mostly null results)

### Menon 2025
- **Source:** Consensus
- **Study Design:** Systematic review
- **Exclusion Reason:** Duplicate of PMID 40959699 (already included from PubMed search). Same paper identified through different search pathway.
- **Decision:** **EXCLUDE** (Criterion: duplicate)
