## Screening Criteria

### Inclusion Criteria
- Human subjects (clinical trials, human observational studies, or reviews of human evidence)
- Oral lutein supplementation or dietary lutein intake (not exclusively topical application)
- Peer-reviewed publications with PMID or verifiable DOI
- Lutein as primary intervention or co-primary alongside zeaxanthin (as in AREDS2)
- Full citation metadata available (title, authors, journal, year, PMID/DOI)

### Exclusion Criteria
- Animal-only studies (unless foundational mechanistic with no human equivalent)
- No PMID and no verifiable DOI
- Topical-only application studies with no relevance to oral supplementation
- Conference abstracts without peer-reviewed publication
- Duplicate citations (same PMID appearing in more than one domain)
- Fabricated or placeholder citations
