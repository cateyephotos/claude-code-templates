# Screening Decisions: NAD+ Precursors (NR / NMN) (ID 25)
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-06 | Mode: Mode 2 — Evidence Update

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (clinical/epidemiological); in vitro/mechanistic reviews acceptable in mechanisms section with explicit labeling
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal with verifiable DOI and PMID
3. Directly relevant to NR, NMN, or NAD+ supplementation, pharmacology, or safety
4. Original research article, systematic review, meta-analysis, or clinical practice guideline
5. PMID resolves to the claimed paper (verified via PubMed MCP)
6. Findings accurately extractable from abstract

### Exclusion Criteria
1. PMID not found in PubMed or resolves to wrong paper
2. In vitro or animal studies in benefits/safety sections (mechanistic reviews accepted in mechanisms)
3. Multi-ingredient products where NR/NMN contribution cannot be isolated
4. Conference abstracts without full journal publication
5. Non-English without English abstract
6. Retracted papers
7. Duplicate datasets from already-included trials
8. Studies using intravenous NAD+ administration (different pharmacokinetics from oral supplements)
