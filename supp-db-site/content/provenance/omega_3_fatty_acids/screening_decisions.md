# Screening Decisions: Omega-3 Fatty Acids
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-04

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (clinical trials, observational studies, or meta-analyses thereof)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal (2025 or later)
3. Available DOI and PMID (or sufficient identification for Consensus-sourced papers)
4. Relevant to omega-3 fatty acids' claimed benefits, mechanisms, or safety
5. Meta-analysis, systematic review, or large RCT preferred
6. Adds NEW information beyond existing 21 database citations
7. Oral supplementation route (excludes parenteral, topical, or enteral nutrition)

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Conference abstracts only (no full publication)
2. Duplicate publications of same dataset
3. Too specialized/niche for general supplement database (e.g., ICU parenteral nutrition)
4. Non-human route of administration (parenteral, IV lipid emulsions)
5. Animal or in-vitro studies without human data
6. Findings that merely replicate existing coverage without substantially new insights
7. Comment/letter format without original data
