## Inclusion Criteria

Papers were included if they met ALL of the following:

1. **Population:** Human subjects (any age, health status)
2. **Intervention:** Phosphatidylserine supplementation as primary or prominently featured intervention
3. **Study Design:** Systematic review, meta-analysis, network meta-analysis, or large RCT (n >= 50)
4. **Outcomes:** Cognitive function, ADHD symptoms, memory, stress/cortisol, or neuroprotection
5. **Publication Date:** 2024-01-01 to 2026-03-05
6. **Language:** English
7. **Novelty:** Not already present in the existing enhanced citations file

## Exclusion Criteria

Papers were excluded if they met ANY of the following:

1. PS used only as a biomarker or biochemical endpoint (not as a supplement)
2. PS is a minor component of a multicomponent formula with no PS-specific outcomes
3. Animal-only or in-vitro-only studies
4. Case reports, editorials, commentaries, or conference abstracts
5. Genetic or molecular studies where PS is not supplemented
6. Duplicate of paper already in enhanced citations file
7. Underpowered (n < 30) observational studies
8. PS exposure (e.g., erythrocyte PS externalization) rather than oral supplementation
