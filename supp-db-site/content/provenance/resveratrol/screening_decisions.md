# Screening Decisions: Resveratrol (ID: 27)
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-06

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (clinical trials or meta-analyses thereof)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal with confirmed DOI and PMID
3. Relevant to resveratrol's claimed benefits, mechanisms, or safety
4. Provides quantitative outcome data (effect sizes, WMD, SMD, or study-level statistics)
5. Study type: RCT, meta-analysis, or systematic review

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Animal or in vitro studies only (unless critical for mechanism context — none included here)
2. Non-English without English abstract
3. Retracted papers
4. Conference abstracts only (no full publication)
5. Protocol/design papers without outcome data
6. Papers with ambiguous or unconfirmed PMID/DOI
7. Duplicate publications of the same dataset
