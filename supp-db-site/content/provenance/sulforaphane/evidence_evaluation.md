### Aggregate Quality Metrics

- **RCTs (completed, human):** 7 (Singh 2014; Egner 2014; Zimmerman 2021; Magner 2023; Yuan 2025; Tian 2024 [human arm]; Chartoumpekis 2019; Shapiro 2006 Phase I; Sivapalan 2018; Cramer 2012)
- **Systematic reviews / meta-analyses:** 2 (Clifford 2021 Nrf2 SR; Monteiro 2023 renoprotection MA — preclinical)
- **Cross-lab replication:** Johns Hopkins (Talalay/Fahey/Kensler/Singh), University of Pittsburgh (Yuan/Kensler), University of Massachusetts (Zimmerman), Charles University Prague (Magner), Quadram Institute UK (Sivapalan/Mithen), Lausanne University Hospital (Chartoumpekis/Sykiotis), University of Illinois (Cramer/Jeffery), Wenzhou Medical University China (Tian) — broad geographic and institutional independence
- **Funding diversity:** US DoD (Zimmerman 2021), academic (most), industry-related disclosures present in some trials (Brassica Protection Products LLC for several Hopkins authors)
- **Recency:** 4 of 12 papers from 2024–2025 (Tian 2024, Yuan 2025, plus Magner 2023, Monteiro 2023) — actively expanding evidence base
