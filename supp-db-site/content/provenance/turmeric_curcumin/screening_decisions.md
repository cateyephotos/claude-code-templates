# Screening Decisions: Turmeric/Curcumin
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-04

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (for clinical evidence — in vivo animal data noted when part of hybrid reviews)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal (2025 or later)
3. Available DOI and PMID
4. Relevant to curcumin's claimed benefits, mechanisms, or safety
5. Meta-analysis, systematic review, or large RCT preferred
6. Adds NEW information beyond existing database citations

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Conference abstracts only (no full publication)
2. Duplicate publications of same dataset
3. Too specialized/niche for general supplement database
4. Non-significant primary outcomes without novel insights
5. Comment/letter format without original data
