### Evidence Gaps Identified
1. Limited evidence for curcumin in younger adults (<60) for cognitive outcomes
2. Optimal formulation (piperine-enhanced vs specialized) still under-studied in head-to-head trials
3. Long-term (>1 year) cognitive outcome data remains limited
4. Need for large multi-center RCTs with standardized cognitive batteries
