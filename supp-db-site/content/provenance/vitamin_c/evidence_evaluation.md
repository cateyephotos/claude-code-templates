## GRADE Assessment Framework

Each included study is evaluated across the following GRADE domains:
- **Risk of Bias:** Limitations in study design and execution
- **Inconsistency:** Heterogeneity across included studies within the MA
- **Indirectness:** Applicability of evidence to the target population/intervention
- **Imprecision:** Width of confidence intervals relative to clinical thresholds
- **Publication Bias:** Evidence of selective reporting or missing studies
- **Overall GRADE:** Very Low / Low / Moderate / High

**Evidence contribution:** Weak support for immune/COVID-19 benefit at GRADE Very Low. The contradicting Zhou 2025 study substantially undermines confidence. Retained for completeness but with explicit caveats about evidence inconsistency.
