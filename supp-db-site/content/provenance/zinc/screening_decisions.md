# Screening Decisions: Zinc
## Pipeline Run: 2026-03-04

### Inclusion Criteria
1. Human subjects (for clinical evidence)
2. Published in peer-reviewed journal
3. Available DOI and PMID
4. Zinc-specific evidence (not just a component of multi-supplement study)
5. Published 2025 or later (for update purposes)
6. Meta-analysis or systematic review with quantitative pooled estimates

### Exclusion Criteria
1. Non-English without English abstract
2. Retracted papers
3. Conference abstracts only
4. Multi-nutrient studies without zinc-specific subgroup analysis
5. Narrative reviews without systematic methodology
6. Studies focused on dietary zinc intake rather than supplementation
