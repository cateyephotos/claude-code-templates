**Justification:** Zinc research spans decades with comprehensive mechanistic understanding, hundreds of RCTs, and multiple meta-analyses. The 2025 papers add new application domains to an already mature evidence base.

### Evidence Gaps Identified
1. No new zinc-specific dose-response meta-analysis (unlike Vitamin D3 which has Zhang 2025)
2. Alopecia areata evidence is observational (serum levels), not supplementation RCTs
3. PMS meta-analyses each include only 5 studies — more RCTs needed for robust conclusion
4. No new meta-analysis specifically on zinc and immune function (most recent is pre-2024)
5. Limited evidence on long-term zinc supplementation outcomes (>1 year)
