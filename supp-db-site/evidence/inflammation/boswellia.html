Understanding Inflammation & Joint Health

Chronic inflammation involves sustained NF-κB activation, elevated pro-inflammatory cytokines, COX-2 overexpression, and tissue-level inflammatory cascades affecting joints, cardiovascular, and metabolic health.

Key Physiological Systems

  • NF-κB pathway
  • COX-2/LOX enzymes
  • Cytokine signaling
  • Prostaglandin synthesis

Key Signaling Molecules

  • TNF-α
  • IL-6
  • CRP
  • Prostaglandins
