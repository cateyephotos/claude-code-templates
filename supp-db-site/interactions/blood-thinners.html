Supplements That Interact With Blood Thinners & Anticoagulants

Supplements with antiplatelet or blood-thinning activity can add to the effect of prescription anticoagulants, increasing the risk of bleeding, bruising, and prolonged clotting times.

Why this class matters

Mechanism: Additive antiplatelet/anticoagulant effect — many botanicals inhibit platelet aggregation via COX, thromboxane, or vitamin K pathways.

What to do: If you take a prescription blood thinner, consult your prescriber before starting any supplement in this category. Surgery: stop 1–2 weeks before. Monitor INR more frequently if on warfarin.

Representative drugs:

  • Warfarin (Coumadin)
  • Apixaban (Eliquis)
  • Rivaroxaban (Xarelto)
  • Clopidogrel (Plavix)
  • Heparin
  • Aspirin (antiplatelet dose)
