Supplements That Interact With Stimulant Medications

Caffeine and other stimulant supplements can compound the effects of ADHD medications and other prescription stimulants — raising heart rate, blood pressure, anxiety, and insomnia risk.

Why this class matters

Mechanism: Additive sympathomimetic activity — increased catecholamine release and receptor sensitivity.

What to do: Limit caffeine and other stimulant supplements when starting ADHD medication. Monitor heart rate and blood pressure. Watch for anxiety, insomnia, or jitteriness.

Representative drugs:

  • Methylphenidate (Ritalin, Concerta)
  • Amphetamine/Dextroamphetamine (Adderall)
  • Lisdexamfetamine (Vyvanse)
  • Modafinil (Provigil)
