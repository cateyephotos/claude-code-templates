We respect the doctrine of fair use as defined under 17 U.S.C. § 107. Fair use allows limited use of copyrighted material without permission for purposes such as criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, and research.

Factors considered in fair use include:

  • The purpose and character of the use (commercial vs. educational)
