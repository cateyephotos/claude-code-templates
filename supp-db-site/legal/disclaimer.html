Supplement use involves inherent risks, including but not limited to:

  • Drug interactions — Supplements can interact with prescription medications, over-the-counter drugs, and other supplements. Some interactions can be dangerous or life-threatening.
  • Pre-existing conditions — Certain supplements may be contraindicated for individuals with specific medical conditions, including but not limited to pregnancy, breastfeeding, liver disease, kidney disease, bleeding disorders, autoimmune conditions, and scheduled surgeries.
