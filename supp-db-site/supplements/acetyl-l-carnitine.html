Overview

Acetyl-L-Carnitine is classified as amino acids with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Cochrane review (Hudson 2003) + multiple RCTs for cognitive decline, depression, fatigue. New NMA evidence for ED (Barbonetti 2024, 15 RCTs) and male fertility (Niu 2025, 16 RCTs). Tier 2: multiple RCTs with moderate evidence across domains, Cochrane review exists but is dated (2003).

Well-characterized amino acid with strong mechanistic understanding and moderate evidence for cognitive and metabolic benefits. New NMA evidence supports ALC for erectile dysfunction (SUCRA 97% in combination) and male fertility (sperm quality improvement).

Key study populations: Elderly with cognitive decline; Depression patients; Athletes; Men with erectile dysfunction; Infertile males
