Overview

Alpha-GPC is classified as nootropics with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Multicenter RCT (Jeon 2024, n=100) demonstrates ADAS-cog improvement in MCI; first PROSPERO-registered comparative MA (Sagaro 2025) shows superiority over citicoline; 7+ RCTs across cognitive and athletic domains

Jeon 2024: ADAS-cog decreased 2.34 points vs placebo in MCI (multicenter RCT, n=100, 12 weeks),Sagaro 2025: α-GPC superior to citicoline on SCAG scale (WMD=-3.92, 95% CI: -7.41 to -0.42; SR+MA, 3 RCTs, n=358),Tamura 2021: Motivation enhancement d=0.72 (RCT, n=24),Kawamura 2024: Stroop/N-Back/Flanker improvements (RCT, n=20),Marcus 2017: Dose-dependent performance at 500mg (RCT, n=54)

Key study populations: Cognitive decline patients; Athletes; Students; Mild cognitive impairment (MCI); Dementia patients
