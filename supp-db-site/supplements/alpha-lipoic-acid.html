Overview

Alpha-Lipoic Acid is classified as antioxidants with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Strong evidence for antioxidant effects and diabetic neuropathy; moderate evidence for cognitive benefits

Well-characterized antioxidant compound with strong evidence for metabolic and neuroprotective benefits

Key study populations: Diabetic patients; Neuropathy; Cognitive decline; Metabolic disorders
