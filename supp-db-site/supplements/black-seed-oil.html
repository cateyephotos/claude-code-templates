Overview

Black Seed Oil is classified as herbal extracts with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Multiple studies showing diverse health benefits across systems

Nigella sativa (black seed oil) and its active compound thymoquinone demonstrate strong evidence across multiple meta-analyses of RCTs for improving lipid profiles, reducing blood pressure, attenuating inflammatory and oxidative stress markers, and supporting glycemic control in metabolic syndrome and type 2 diabetes

Key study populations: Metabolic disorders; Inflammatory conditions; Immune support; Respiratory health

Enhancement of humoral immune responses through increased immunoglobulin production
