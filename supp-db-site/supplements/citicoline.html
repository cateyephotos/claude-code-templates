Overview

Citicoline is classified as nootropics with strong evidence (Tier 1) for its primary applications. Multiple high-quality RCTs and meta-analyses demonstrating cognitive benefits

Well-established nootropic with strong evidence for cognitive enhancement and neuroprotection

Key study populations: Cognitive decline; Stroke recovery; Age-related memory loss; Healthy adults
