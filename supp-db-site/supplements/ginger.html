Overview

Ginger is classified as herbal extracts with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Tier 2: Two meta-analyses establish pooled evidence anchors (NVP meta-analysis N=1,278 Viljoen 2014; OA meta-analysis N=593 Bartels 2015). Dual COX/LOX inhibition mechanism well-established. Third metabolic meta-analysis N=454 (Zhu 2018). Tier 1 not reached: no Cochrane review, no single landmark RCT N≥500, GRADE MODERATE evidence level.

Well-established herbal supplement with strong evidence for anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits

Key study populations: Nausea sufferers; Inflammation; Digestive issues; General wellness

Nausea and vomiting relief in pregnancy and chemotherapy
