Overview

HMB (β-Hydroxy β-Methylbutyrate) is classified as performance enhancers with preliminary evidence (Tier 3) for its primary applications. Moderate evidence for muscle preservation and recovery, particularly in untrained or elderly populations

Performance supplement with moderate evidence for muscle preservation and recovery enhancement

Key study populations: Untrained individuals; Elderly populations; Athletes in caloric deficit; Clinical populations

Mechanisms of Action

1

mTOR pathway activation for protein synthesis

2

Ubiquitin-proteasome pathway inhibition (reduced protein breakdown)

3

Cholesterol synthesis for cell membrane repair

4

Leucine metabolite with anti-catabolic properties

Benefits

Cognitive Benefits

  • Indirect neuroprotection via reduced systemic inflammation

Non-Cognitive Benefits

  • Muscle protein synthesis
  • Muscle preservation
  • Recovery enhancement
  • Lean mass support
