Overview

Huperzine A is classified as nootropics with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Strong evidence for acetylcholinesterase inhibition; moderate evidence for memory enhancement

Nootropic compound with strong mechanistic understanding for acetylcholinesterase inhibition but limited clinical data

Key study populations: Alzheimer's disease; Age-related cognitive decline; Memory impairment; Students
