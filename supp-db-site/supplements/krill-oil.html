Overview

Krill Oil is classified as essential nutrients with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Clinical studies showing enhanced omega-3 bioavailability and benefits

Well-characterized omega-3 source with strong evidence for cardiovascular health and enhanced bioavailability

Key study populations: Cardiovascular health; Joint pain; Cognitive support; Eye health
