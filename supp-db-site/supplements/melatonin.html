Background

Overview

Melatonin is classified as sleep support with strong evidence (Tier 1) for its primary applications. A naturally occurring hormone secreted by the pineal gland, it serves as the body's primary circadian timing signal.

Well-characterized sleep aid with extensive high-quality evidence for sleep quality and circadian rhythm regulation.

Key study populations: Insomnia patients; Shift workers; Elderly; Jet lag sufferers
