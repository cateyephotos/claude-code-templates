Overview

Milk Thistle is classified as herbal extracts with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Extensive clinical evidence for hepatoprotective effects

Multiple meta-analyses consistently demonstrate silymarin hepatoprotective effects with significant reductions in liver enzymes and improvement in NAFLD outcomes across over 2000 patients

Key study populations: Liver disease; Hepatitis; Fatty liver
