Overview

Piracetam is classified as nootropics with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Extensive clinical research with mixed but generally positive results

Original nootropic compound with moderate evidence for cognitive enhancement and neuroprotection

Key study populations: Age-related cognitive decline; Dementia; Stroke recovery; Learning enhancement
