Overview

Spirulina is classified as antioxidants with moderate evidence (Tier 2) for its primary applications. Strong evidence for nutritional benefits and antioxidant effects; moderate evidence for cognitive support

Well-characterized superfood with strong antioxidant mechanisms and moderate evidence for immune and cardiovascular benefits

Key study populations: Malnutrition; Athletes; Cardiovascular disease; Immune support
