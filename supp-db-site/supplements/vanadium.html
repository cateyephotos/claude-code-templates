Overview

Vanadium is classified as essential nutrients with preliminary evidence (Tier 3) for its primary applications. Limited but promising human studies for glucose metabolism

Essential trace mineral with moderate evidence for glucose metabolism support but limited clinical data

Key study populations: Type 2 diabetics; Insulin-resistant individuals
