Overview

Vinpocetine is classified as nootropics with preliminary evidence (Tier 3) for its primary applications. Moderate evidence for cerebral circulation; limited evidence for cognitive enhancement in healthy individuals

Nootropic compound with strong mechanistic understanding for cerebral blood flow but limited clinical evidence

Key study populations: Stroke patients; Cognitive decline; Hearing loss; Vascular dementia
