Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Stack Analyzer work?

The Stack Analyzer uses Claude AI to evaluate your supplement selections against a specific health goal. It analyzes pairwise interactions, maps biological mechanism coverage, flags safety concerns, and generates an optimized dosage protocol — all grounded in clinical evidence from our database of 93 evidence-rated supplements.

How many analyses do I get?

Free accounts include 3 analyses per month. Subscribers get 25 analyses per month. Credits reset on the 1st of each month. Each analysis uses one credit regardless of depth (Quick, Standard, or Deep).

What do the analysis depths mean?

Quick — a concise overview with overall score, top interactions, and critical safety flags (~5 seconds).
Standard — thorough analysis covering all sections including mechanism coverage and dosage protocol (~10 seconds).
Deep — comprehensive analysis with detailed mechanism mapping, full synergy analysis, and complete dosage timing windows (~15 seconds).

Is this medical advice?

No. The Stack Analyzer is an informational tool that synthesizes published research. It does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting or modifying any supplement regimen, especially if you take prescription medications.

What is the evidence tier system?

Tier 1 (Strong) — Multiple large RCTs, systematic reviews, or meta-analyses.
Tier 2 (Moderate) — Several RCTs with moderate sample sizes, consistent results.
Tier 3 (Limited) — Few small trials, preliminary evidence, or mostly preclinical data.

Can I enter custom supplements?

No. To ensure accurate, reproducible analyses and control costs, all inputs are constrained to our curated database of 93 evidence-rated supplements. This prevents hallucinated recommendations and keeps token usage predictable. If you'd like a supplement added, contact us via the newsletter.
