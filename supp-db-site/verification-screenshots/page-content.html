Evidence Standards

Our database is built on rigorous evidence standards, analyzing 471+ research papers from the last 20 years.

  • Tier 1: Meta-analyses and systematic reviews
  • Tier 2: Large RCTs (>100 participants)
  • Tier 3: Smaller RCTs (30-100 participants)
  • Tier 4: Preliminary studies and animal research

Target Audiences

Designed for healthcare professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and informed consumers seeking evidence-based guidance.

  • Fitness enthusiasts

  • General wellness consumers
